About Me

I am Ashyel Feie M. Briones an aspiring web developer and highly motivated third-year Computer Science student at St. Paul University at San Miguel. Eager to gain hands-on experience through on-the-job training, I aim to apply my theoretical knowledge to real-world projects and deepen my understanding of modern web development technologies. I have practical experience building applications using Eclipse IDE and MySQL databases, and designing user-friendly prototypes with Figma. Known for strong analytical skills, effective communication, and a collaborative mindset, I am committed to continuous learning and growth in the fast-evolving web development industry.

Acacia's Tables and Chairs Rental Application

  • Developed a desktop-based rental application using Java Swing Components and MySQL Database for Acacia’s Tables and Chairs.
  • The application is designed for individual users and small-scale businesses, featuring user login, item listing, and rental transactions.

  • Focused on UI/UX design principles to ensure the platform is visually appealing and easy to navigate for potential online customers.
